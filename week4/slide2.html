• netfilter/iptables are the linux NAT/pf & pm system
  • NAT: Network Address Translation
  • pf: Packet Filtering
  • pm: Packet Mangling
  • successor to ipchains (2.2.x) and ipfwadm (2.0.x)
  • netfilter is the portion that goes in the kernel
  • iptables is table structure and command for defining firewall rules
