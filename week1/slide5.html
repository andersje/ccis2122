• shutdown is commonly used to down the system
  • Under linux and BSD, -r reboots and -h halts
  • generally, no flag means goto single-user
  • When is required: Such as 'shutdown -r now'
  • time can be specified in +minutes, e.g. +5
  • warning messages sent every hour when > 3 hours
    every 30 minutes when > 1 hour
    every 15 minutes when > 10 minutes
    every 1 minute thereafter
